About Candra

  • Home/
  • About
My name is Candra — friends call me Candy. Last name's Fried (pronounced | freed). I'm a UIX Design Engineer, Developer & Visual Artist based in Desoto. I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas and moved to Philadelphia many moons ago for school. I attended Moore College of Art and Design where I majored in Illustration and Graphic Design. I furthered my education learning server-side web development at the University of the Arts, and later earned an Applications Development Certificate from LaSalle University. In 2014, I earned my Masters in Computer Information Science at Penn State, Great Valley.

I specialize in a variety of creative services ranging from professional illustrations, print-work to web development. I'm proficient in the full Adobe Creative Suite, HTML 5, CSS, Javascript and several other graphic programs, applications and frameworks. If you're in need of a new design, redesign, or if you'd like to commission any artwork, don't hesitate to get in touch. Or just say hello for no good reason.
