← DDoS Services Portal

Comcast's DDoS Services Portal (DSP) orchestrates the onboarding and maintenance of commercial DDoS customers. DSP pulls together almost a dozen different Comcast platforms in order to simplify and streamline the process of onboarding, and maintaining new customers while generating useful KPIs to drive growth. Lifecycle: Conceptual meeting with stakeholders, Logo Design (Photoshop/Illustrator), Wireframe/Sketch (Axure), Front-End Development (Angular, Typescript, Bulma, Material.io, Reactive Forms, Insomnia, Git, and Cloud Foundry)
